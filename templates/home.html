FAQ - Edeypay

How does the lottery work on this website?
Simply register with your full name, mobile number, age, and account number. Fund your account with a minimum of ₦1,000, start playing with a minimum of ₦200. Pick two numbers, and once one of your numbers is picked by the system, you have won. The odds are 20, which means you can win 20 times your initial bet.
How can I participate in the lottery?
You can participate by simply registering, funding your account with just ₦1,000, and start playing with a minimum of ₦200.
Is this website legal and regulated?
This website is being regulated by the Nigerian National Lottery Commission.
What are the odds of winning a prize?
The odds are 20, which means that if you play with ₦500, you win ₦10,000. If you play with ₦1,000, you win ₦20,000, and if you play with ₦50,000, you win ₦1,000,000.
How are winners determined and notified?
At the end of every game time, the system automatically generates five numbers from 1 to 77. If your number is part of the numbers that the system picked, you have won. The five numbers are then published on the top right corner of our website.
What happens if I win?
Your money will be added to your balance, and you can withdraw it anytime.
How do I withdraw my money?
You can withdraw your money anytime, any day once you reach a withdrawal threshold of ₦5,000 only. Once you make a withdrawal, you will see your money inside your bank account almost immediately.
Are there age or location restrictions for participation?
The age restriction is 18. If you are under 18, you cannot register on our website, and there's no location barrier to use our website.
What payment methods are accepted on this website?
We accept almost all payment methods that you can think of, which is why you can play Edeypay lottery games from anywhere in the world.
Is my personal and financial information secure on this website?
All your personal and financial information is well secure on our website. We don't sell, trade, or give out any of your information for any reason.
Can I play the lottery from outside of Nigeria?
Yes, you can play Edeypay lottery games from anywhere in the world.
What happens to unwithdrawn money?
Your unwithdrawn money will be in your account and will be readily available for withdrawal anytime, any day, so long as you can still log in to your account.
Are taxes withheld from my winnings?
No taxes or percentages or hidden charges of any kind are deducted from your money, even when making a withdrawal. Your money is paid in full.
Can I play multiple games at once?
Yes, you can play up to five games at once, but not more than five. If caught playing more than five games at once and you win, you might not be paid.
How can I check the results of past lottery draws?
Results are always displayed on the right side top of our website, and if your number was picked, your winnings will be added to your money.
Can I cancel or refund my ticket purchase?
No, there is no cancellation of tickets once purchased.
Do you offer any promotions or bonuses for players?
Yes, we always offer promotions and bonuses, so always stay in touch with all our social media platforms and turn on notifications for our website on your device.
How can I contact customer support for assistance?
Call or text or WhatsApp 08102437371 or send an Email to customersupport@edeypay.money, aiictcenter@gmail.com.
Is there a minimum age requirement to use this website?
The minimum age to use our website is 18+.
What if I encounter technical issues while using the website?
Kindly contact our customer service, and it will be fixed immediately.
